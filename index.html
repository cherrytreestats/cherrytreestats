Cherry Tree Activity

After a long 1.5 years, we finally hit 50.000 messages - an outstandingly meaningless milestone!

Still, I took it as an incentive to have a look at our past activity. Why, you may ask? Well, because numbers are fun, right? ... right?!
Anyway! The server itself was created around October 2020 and has seen various degrees of activity :') We've had a lot of ups and downs, many stagnant months with barely any messages, but in the end we always come back.
Most notably, four distinct peaks can be observed in the left graph. Not surprisingly, the server saw a lot of activity right after it was created. I'm sure that for some of us the server felt most alive at that time. It's important to mention is that we've had various other members across the years. Even though some have not left fond memories, they helped make the server what it is that! Unfortunately I cannot include them in these statistics because discord's search function only indexes members that are currently on the server.

The second peak, starting around February 2021, marks one of my favourite times with you guys: The Cherry Tree Minecraft Server :]
I honestly loved seeing everyone come together to play and have fun. Almost the entire server was participating, even Drew and Alex joined in. We spent many hours in our Minecraft world and even today I still enjoy looking at the stuff we built ^^
Good times!

It's quite funny that the most active time on our small Overwatch Server was actually caused by a completely different game. I couldn't believe it at first, but it seems like Pokemon Go dominated the server during summer 2021 :D To be fair, we weren't only catching pidgeys and hatching useless 7km eggs.. we did play a lot of Overwatch as well. This becomes evident when looking at Steqs' high activity during Pokemon Go season although he never played the game. But I'm still convinced we never would've seen the server bloom the same way if it hadn't been for Pokemon Go!

Finally, the most recent peak was around Christmas and although it can't be deduced from the data, I can guarantee that the sole reason was Sakura's reappearance. After all, she's the person that brought us all together. It makes sense that the activity of most of us will increase whenever she's active! Also.. if you're reading this, Sakura: We appreciate you and (at least I) don't mind it when you're absent for a while. You do you, we've established quite early on that a low participation was not a direct problem. Right, Rickard? :P Anyway, feel free to join us whenever :)

... oh btw, maybe I'm just on dangerously high levels of copium but I'm starting to see a fifth peak emerging from the end of april :^)
